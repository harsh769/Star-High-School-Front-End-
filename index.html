Classes We Offer

Our School Provides the Education from Kindergarten to Twelfth Grade.

SUB JUNIOR

Sub junior classes in our school typically refer to the early years of education for children,ranging from kindergarten or pre-school up to fourth grade. During these years, the focus is on developing basic skills in reading, writing, and math.

JUNIOR

Junior classes in our school typically refer to the middle years of education for children, ranging from fifth grade up to eighth grade. During these years, the focus is on building upon the foundational skills developed in sub junior classes

SENIOR

Senior classes in our school typically refer to the final years of high school education, ranging from ninth to twelfth grade. During these years, the focus is on preparing students for college, career, and life beyond high school.
